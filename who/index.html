My name is Josefina Muñoz. Born in 1995 in Bogotá, Colombia, currently a fashion design student at Parsons School of Design, NYC. My work has been focused on social sustainability and justice, where I have tried to communicate a profound love and a deep distrust of the world we inhabit.
